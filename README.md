# Escape the Abandoned School

## Overview

**Escape the Abandoned School** is a first-person horror puzzle game where players must explore a deserted school, collect important items, unlock restricted areas, survive hazards and obtain the Master Key in order to escape.

---

## How to Run the Application

1. Extract the ZIP file
2. Launch the executable (`.exe`) file
3. Wait for the game to load
4. Click **Start** to begin playing
5. Follow the on-screen instructions and interact with objects to progress through the game

## Gameplay Objective

The objective of the game is to escape the haunted school.

To complete the game, players must:

1. Collect all **4 collectibles**.
2. Unlock the **Staff Room**.
3. Find and collect the **Master Key**.
4. Use the Master Key to unlock the **Escape Door**.
5. Escape the school successfully.

---

## Puzzle Walkthrough / Answer Key

### Collectibles

The player must collect the following items found in the classroom:

- Student ID (at the back, close to the chair)
- Book (on a table)
- File (under a table)
- Pencil Case (in the trash bin)

### Staff Room

- The Staff Room remains locked until all **4 collectibles** have been collected.
- Once all collectibles are obtained, the Staff Room will unlock.

### Master Key

- The Master Key is hidden inside a locker within the Staff Room.
- Interact with the correct locker to reveal and collect the key.

### Escape Door

- After obtaining the Master Key, proceed to the Escape Door.
- Press **E** to escape and complete the game.

---

## Hazards

### Monster Jumpscare

Entering the Staff Room trigger area will activate a monster jumpscare.

Effects:
- Player health immediately drops to **0**.
- A death screen is displayed.
- The player respawns at the corridor checkpoint.
- The monster disappears after the respawn.

### Monster Residue (Puddle Hazard)

Stepping into the puddle hazard will:

- Continuously decrease player health.
- Display a warning message.
- Stop dealing damage once the player leaves the puddle.

## Known Limitations / Bugs

1. The monster jumpscare can only be triggered once.
2. Health resets to full after respawning from the monster encounter.
3. The game currently contains only one playable level.
4. No save/load system is implemented.
